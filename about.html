About Us

We are an organization based in Gurgaon. Started in 2020, we aim towards providing low cost education of Total Productive Maintenance so that you can help your organization to reduce the losses in the production and take your organization on the new heights.

We provide the Online learning to the people using the android application. You can go to the application and have a free trial and explore the various features and functionality of the app.

How We Started?

One day sitting together we were trying to find a better way to increase the productivity by investing less in human resources. We came to conclusion to hire or train people for TPM but the resource and training was too costly and it was a problem everywhere so we decided to launch an android app where you can learn everything about TPM and even gain the practical knowledge of TPM. Where you can consult our 3experts or any one in tour community to get solved your problem. So here we present the TPM World Services an android app which will help you in every concept of TPM and will increase your and yours organization value in the market. The package anyone can afford be it an individual or an organization.
